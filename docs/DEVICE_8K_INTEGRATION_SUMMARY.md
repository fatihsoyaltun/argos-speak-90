# Device 8K Integration Summary

## Purpose

This document summarizes the Phase Device-3 micro curriculum integration for the ForenScope 8K Android Latent Fingerprint Detection Tablet.

The integration keeps Argos Speak 90 as a general speaking-first English curriculum. The 8K material appears only as small work/technical contexts on selected days.

## What Changed By Module

### Listening

Each selected day now includes a short 8K work/technical micro-context:

- Day 48: a technician asks someone to check the selected light group and filter.
- Day 57: a teammate exported an image in the wrong format.
- Day 64: a contactless workflow moves from capture to archive/report.
- Day 70: a polite reminder to use the darkroom for UVC mode.
- Day 71: a customer demo priority is selected.
- Day 80: a work decision connects report/export and AFIS workflow.
- Day 85: two options are given when fingerprint detail is unclear.
- Day 88: a technical observation is summarized with date stamp/geotag language.

### Words

Each selected day keeps exactly 8 vocabulary items after the existing expansion logic. The base day content now includes 1-3 source-backed 8K terms, while the remaining items stay useful for general speaking.

### Speaking

Each selected day now asks for a realistic speaking action:

- clarify
- give feedback
- explain a process
- give a polite safety instruction
- choose a demo priority
- make a work decision
- present options
- summarize a technical observation

Second-try improvement remains part of the speaking flow.

### Review

Each selected day now includes a short production-style review item connected to the 8K micro-context, while the global review wrapper still preserves at least one production-style review item per day.

## Risks Avoided

- No SuperSpectral content was added.

- No guarantee wording was added.

- The curriculum remains speaking-first instead of becoming a device brochure.
